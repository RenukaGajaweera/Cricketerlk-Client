Rules of The Game

Basic Rules

  • 11 Players has every match has to be selected.
  • Players will be divided as batsman , all-rounders and bowlers.
  • Minimum of 01 wicket keeper per team needs to be selected.
  • Maximum of 08 players from one team can be selected per game.
  • Selection of players has to be done before the official time of the toss
  • Points will be counted if 20 overs or more played in the match in case of abandonment.
  • Leaderboard will indicate cumulative standing after each match.
  • League is open to all. However prizes will be awarded only to Sri Lankans
  • Final decision on prizes will be at the discretion of the management of cricketerlk.com.

Points System

Batting Points System
  • Every 1 run = 1 points
  • Not out bonus = 10 points (minimum 20 balls )
  • Duck/Golden Duck = -5 points (Not for Bowlers)
  • Boundries :
    • Every 4 = 1 points
    • Every 6 = 3 points
  • Batting Milestone Bonuses :
    • Between 25-49 runs = 10 points
    • Between 50-74 runs = 25 points
    • Between 75-99 runs = 40 points
    • Between 100-124 runs = 60 points
    • Between 125-149 runs = 75 points
    • 150 or above = 90 points
  • Batting Milestone Bonuses :
    • Between 5-9 boundaries = 10 points
    • Between 10-14 boundaries = 15 points
    • 15 or more boundaries = 25 points
  • Strike rates Bonus/penalty (10 balls minimum) :
    • Unit = (No of Runs - Bowls Faced)
    • If positive 1x into number of units is added
    • If negative 0.5x into number of units is deducted
Bowling Points System
  • Each wicket grants 25 points to the bowler
  • Wicket Bonuses :
    • 2 wickets grants 10 bonus points
    • 3 wickets grants 20 bonus points
    • 4 wickets grants 35 bonus points
    • 5 wickets grants 50 bonus points
    • 6 or more wickets grants 50 + 20 bonus points each
  • Economy rate Bonus/Penalty :
    • Unit = (No of Bowls Bowled - No of Runs Scored)
    • If positive 1x into number of units is added
    • If negative 1x into number of units is deducted
  • Hattrick earns 30 bonus points
  • Every maiden over earns 10 bonus points
Fielding Points System
  • Catches grants 10 bonus points each
  • Each Stumping grants 20 bonus points
  • Run Out earn 15 bonus points
Special Rules
  • A captain must be appointed for each team. Total points obtained by the captain is multiplied by a factor of 2 (Total points X 2)
  • A vice captain must be appointed for each team. Total points obtained by the vice captain is multiplied by a factor of 1.5 (Total points X 1.5)
  • Man of the match earns 50 bonus points
